Notes & Tips

  • outer = inner + gap keeps the perceived curvature consistent when padding changes. This is handy for cards with fixed inner radius and variable gutters.
  • r = k · min(h, w) scales smoothly across sizes. Try k between 0.12 and 0.2 for balanced capsules, or up to 0.5 for full pill shapes.
  • The preview uses device pixels; sub‑pixel rounding may alter exact integers at render time.
